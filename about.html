BG Masters Accounting and Tax Services is a young, ambitious East London firm helping businesses grow through tailored tax, accounting and advisory solutions.

Our Story

Why we do this work

BG Masters Accounting and Tax Services is an accounting firm managed by a young and ambitious group of people. Registered in 2019 and based in Southernwood, East London, we've built a practice around one idea: that good accounting should help a business move forward, not just look back.

We invest the time to understand each client's vision, challenges and opportunities, and then tailor the tax, accounting and consulting solutions that fit. Historically, accountants worked by looking in the rear-view mirror at the last reporting period — we'd rather sit alongside you and help shape what comes next.
